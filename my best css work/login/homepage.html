About Us

The Campus Ambassador program is our endeavour to make an innovative network of students around the nation and give them abundant opportunities to ignite the budding entrepreneurial spirits. The chosen Campus Ambassadors will work in cooperation with the team and represent E-Cell, NIT Bhopal in their respective colleges. The program will give them the space to improve their range of abilities while at the same time achieving pioneering progression in their colleges.
Entrepreneurship Cell NIT-Bhopal is a non-profit student organization that works to support and unleash the budding entrepreneurial minds and promote the start-up culture among the students.
We at E-Cell link students with mentors, seekers with investors and aspiring entrepreneurs with enduring entrepreneurs. By organizing several entrepreneurship development programmes and camps, skill development workshops and speaker sessions, hosting brainstorming competitions, we aim to promote the development of knowledge-based enterprises and open doors towards opportunities in inventive areas.
Connected with a prominent alumni network and established entrepreneurs, E-Cell NIT-B thrives to serve the purpose of providing necessary resources and potential guidance to spirited individuals.

Why CA Programme

  1. Skill Development:-Gaining a chance to work with the hard core aspiring team of E-CELL NIT-B, will bring in in you the professionalism and major skills required in the corporate world. It will give a multidimensional growth to your personality, helping you outshine.
  2. Certificate of leadership:- After the successful completion of tenure, the CA will receive a certificate appreciating his/her involvement. Besides getting recognized by the college of national importance, the certificate will enhance your resume.
  3. Organise events:- Organize various entrepreneurship-related events in your college in collaboration with E-Cell NIT-B. This will give you a great first-hand experience of event management and will also introduce you to the creative side of yours.
  4. Get updated:- Stay revised about the corporate world and develop a better understanding and possibilities of the intersection of technology, entrepreneurship and finance
  5. Internship opportunity:- Win a chance to get an internship in top companies.
